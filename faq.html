FAQ

What is affiliate marketing?

Affiliate marketing is performance-based marketing, whereby a product or service gets remunerated
for every sale sent to the merchant.

Does it cost me anything to become an affiliate in your Affiliate program?

There is No Charge required to become our affiliate.

How does Train Togaf affiliate program work?

An Affiliate program is a service allowing participating individuals/websites to earn commissions on sales referred from links clicked through text links, specific product images, buttons, or banners during their marketing campaigns. We provide you with a large selection of banners and other marketing materials and when a visitor you’ve referred to us makes a purchase, you earn a commission of 30% from each sale made and recurring commissions as soon as your referral renews his package.

Is my Website Eligible for the Affiliate program?

We accept almost all types of websites, apart from websites that;

  • Promote or Encourage violence
  • Promote or Encourage sexual explicit material
  • Promote or Encourage Illegal activities
  • Violate intellectual property rights
  • Promote or Encourage sexual orientation or sex, religion, nationality, disability, discrimination based on race.

All applications are welcome but are subject for review before approval.

What are cookies, and why are they important?

Affiliate sales are normally tracked using cookies (small text files set on the visitor’s computer) and the duration of how long to keep them on a visitor’s machine is called cookie life. In Affiliate marketing it means the time period between the click on the affiliate site and the last day we are willing to pay that affiliate a commission of the sale made by their visitor/referral.
We offer a 60 days “life of a click,” which basically means that if anyone buys something from us within 60 days after clicking on your link, we will pay you a commission (unless they’ve clicked on someone else’s affiliate link in between yours and the sale).

What are my responsibilities as a Train Togaf Affiliate Partner?

You are only responsible for using links and creative (marketing materials)
provided by us that enable you to create and place links on your site or other channels listed in our terms.
We handle everything else.

I manage/own more than one website; do I need to apply separately for each?

NO, you do not need to apply separately. If you have multiple websites we recommend
managing them with one account using our tracking tools. We allow you to track the progress of your sites or
pages and get paid a single commission payout.

Whom should I contact if I have questions about Train Togaf Affiliate Program?

For questions about the Train Togaf Affiliate program, please contact Alec Mwali the Affiliate Manager
at affiliates@traintogaf.com

After I join the affiliate program, how do I get the ads and links to put onto my site?

Login to your Affiliate account, go to the “Affiliate Materials,” section, choose any of our creative
options. Choose which link or banner ad you would like to place on your site, copy the HTML code provided below the
banner of your choice and then simply paste it into your site.

Where may I place these affiliate links on my site?

Please place your Affiliate link where they’re easy for your visitors to
find: Your site’s home page, navigation bar, and any other high traffic areas. Placing your links on
easily accessible site locations will more likely lead to a purchase.

Is there a certain number of links I am obliged to place on my website?

No, you may use as many links as you like. We encourage you to try a variety of links to learn
which will increase your click-rates and commission earnings.

Can I earn commissions on my own purchases?

NO

What about product returns/refunds?

If a customer’s refund request is granted, or if credit card charges are reversed due
to a dispute or credit card fraud, your account will be debited for any commission earned on that transaction.

Who processes orders and handles customer service?

We handle all sales, order processing, and customer
service issues for any individual you refer to our site. All you need to do is to promote our
products and services through your link and we will take care of the rest.

How will the merchant know that the orders came from my site?

The link that you set up on your site identifies you as having referred the customer.
We track all related information for the customers which you refer to our site.
